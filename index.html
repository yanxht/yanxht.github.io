Welcome

This is my personal website, where you can find information on my current research and professional development. I am currently working as a Data Scientist at Azure Compute Insights which builds analytics tools for large-scale cloud infrastructure.

I earned my PhD in Statistics from Cornell University. My research centered on using convex optimization to solve structured feature selection problems in high dimensions. While working at Microsoft I got involved in developing scalable network models for networks with millions of nodes (check out our proposal). I also volunteer to provide statistical consulting for non-profit initiatives: collaborating with Microsoft AI for Good, we published studies on the sudden unexpected infant death (SUID) in the US, e.g., its geographic variation, the impact of high altitude.
