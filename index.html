Welcome

This is my personal website where you can find information on my current research and professional development. I am working as a Data Scientist building analytics tools for large-scale cloud infrastructure at Azure. I earned my PhD in Statistics from Cornell University in 2018. My graduate research centered on using convex optimization to solve structured feature selection problems in high dimensions. Some highlighted research I've been involved at Microsoft includes a scalable network model for networks with millions of nodes.

Education

  • PhD in Statistics, Cornell University, 2018, advised by Jacob Bien
  • BS in Statistics and Applied Math, University of Washington, 2013
  • BA in Economics, University of Washington, 2013

Other positions

  • Reviewer for peer-reviewed statistics journals including Electronic Journal of Statistics, Computational Statistics and Data Analysis, Biostatistics, Statistics in Medicine.
  • Statistical consultant for Microsoft AI for Good. Check out our published studies on the sudden unexpected infant death (SUID) in the US, e.g., its geographic variation and the risk of high altitude.
