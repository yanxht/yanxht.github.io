Welcome

This is my personal website, where you can find information on my current research and professional development. I am currently working as a Data Scientist at Azure Compute Insights which builds analytics tools for large-scale cloud infrastructure.

I received my PhD in Statistics from Cornell University. My research centered on using convex optimization to solve structured feature selection problems in high dimensions. While working at Microsoft I got involved in developing scalable network models for networks with millions of nodes (check out our proposal). Outside of work, I volunteer and provide statistical modeling support for non-profit initiatives: collaborating with researchers from Microsoft AI for Good and Seattle Children's Hospital, we published a paper on the geographic variation of sudden unexpected infant death (SUID) across the US.
